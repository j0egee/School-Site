While working I was able to gain skills such as the ability to multitask, problem-solving abilities, and improve in customer service. Whilst at the cash register I was able to assist customers with any questions they have and get a better understanding of the work process. Stepping up to making drinks allowed me to get better at multitasking by working on many different orders at a time. Bagging food altogether was a huge advancement and required me to be able to accurately prepare many orders at the same time as well as adapt to order changes.
